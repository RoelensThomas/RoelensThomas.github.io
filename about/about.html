About

I’m a passionated information designer who develops products that enhance knowledge transfer. My works ranges from insightful visualisations through user-friendly interfaces and powerfull identities. I care about content. As a co-author I’ll search for the story behind your data and as an editor I’ll enforce your story with visual means. My goal is to inform, explain and enlighten people. Therefore the user-experience is always a key factor in any design decision. I translate predefined paramaters into a intrinsic form.
